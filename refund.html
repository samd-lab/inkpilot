Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

The short version:

Don't love your designs? Email us within 24 hours. We'll regenerate them for free, OR refund you in full. No arguments. Tattoos are forever; the design has to feel right.

1. Single Pack ($9)

If you purchased a Single Pack and are unhappy with all 8 designs:

  • Within 24 hours of generation: Email hello@tattoodesignr.com with your purchase email and the issue. We'll either:
    • Regenerate your set with refined prompts (free), or
    • Issue a full refund to your original payment method (typically 3–5 business days to appear on your card)
  • After 24 hours: Refunds reviewed case-by-case. We'll usually still help you.

2. Weekend Unlimited ($19)

The 48-hour Weekend Unlimited pass is fully refundable:

  • If you've used 3 or fewer generations and are unhappy → full refund within 48 hours of purchase.
  • If you've already generated more than 3 sets → no refund (you've consumed the value).

3. Studio License ($99/month)

The monthly subscription is refundable on a pro-rated basis:

  • First month: Full refund within 7 days of initial subscription, no questions asked.
  • Cancellation: Cancel anytime. You retain access through the end of your paid period. We do not auto-bill after cancellation.
  • Pro-rated refunds: If you cancel mid-month and have used the service less than 5 times, we can pro-rate. Email us.

4. What we don't refund

  • Purchases where the user violated Terms of Service (e.g., generating prohibited content).
  • Disputes raised more than 60 days after purchase.
  • Costs incurred at your tattoo artist (we don't refund the price you paid them; that's between you and them).
  • Subscription periods already fully consumed.

5. Chargebacks

If you have an issue, please email us first — we'll fix it. Initiating a chargeback through your bank without contacting us first triggers Stripe's dispute process and can result in a hold on future purchases. We've never lost a chargeback because we honor refund requests directly. Just talk to us.

6. How to request a refund

Email hello@tattoodesignr.com with:

  • The email address you used at checkout.
  • The approximate date and time of your purchase.
  • A brief reason (so we can improve — but you don't have to justify yourself).

We respond within 24 hours, usually faster. Refunds processed within 3 business days, appearing on your card statement within 5–7 business days depending on your bank.

7. Currency & processor fees

Refunds are issued in the original currency of your purchase. We absorb all Stripe processing fees on refunds — you receive 100% of what you paid us.

Contact

hello@tattoodesignr.com — we read every email and respond personally.
