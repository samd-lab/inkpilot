FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does the AI tattoo generator work?+

Type your idea in plain English. Pick from 10 fine-tuned styles. Our AI generates 8 unique designs in ~30 seconds plus on-body previews. Each style runs on its own LoRA — Traditional uses Sailor Jerry palette, Japanese uses Horiyoshi III tebori, Fine Line uses single-needle precision.

How does my tattoo artist use this?+

After paying you get a complete pack: HD PNG (3000×3000), transparent PNG for stencil prep, print-ready stencil PDF, and a spec sheet with recommended size, placement, and the original prompt. Walk in with everything your artist needs.

Can I see how it looks on my body before getting it?+

Yes — every Triple Pack and Studio plan includes on-body mockups rendered on real forearm, calf, back and chest references with multiple camera angles, so you can visualize size and placement before committing.

What if I want a design from your gallery?+

Our $5 Pre-made tier lets you pick any of the 95+ gallery designs and get the full unlock pack — HD, transparent, stencil, spec sheet. Cheapest way to get a polished tattoo design ready for your artist.

Is my idea private?+

Your prompts are deleted within 24 hours. We never share, sell, or use your ideas to train models. Designs are 100% yours with full commercial rights.

Can I use these designs commercially?+

Yes. You own the designs you generate. Use them for your own tattoo, sell flash sheets, license to other artists — full commercial rights, no attribution required.
