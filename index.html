FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does the AI tattoo generator work?+

Type your tattoo idea in plain English. Pick from 10 fine-tuned styles. Our AI generates 8 unique designs in ~30 seconds. Each style has its own model weights — Traditional uses Sailor Jerry palette, Japanese uses Horiyoshi III tebori aesthetic, Fine Line uses single-needle precision.

Can I take the design to my tattoo artist?+

Yes. After paying $9 you get HD PNG (3000×3000), transparent PNG for stencil prep, a print-ready stencil PDF, and the original prompt. Walk into the shop with everything your artist needs.

Do you preview the tattoo on my body?+

On the Triple Pack and Studio plans, yes — body placement preview shows your design on a forearm/calf/back silhouette so you can visualize size and placement.

Is my idea private?+

Yes. No login. No DB. We delete your prompt after 24 hours. Your tattoo idea stays yours.

What if I don't like the designs?+

100% money-back guarantee. If your tattoo artist can't use any of the 8 designs, email hello@tattoodesignr.com and we'll refund you, no questions asked.

How is this different from Pinterest?+

Pinterest gives you ideas you can't legally use (someone else's art). We generate original designs from your specific prompt, in your specific style, with full commercial rights.
