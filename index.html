FAQ

Questions, answered.

Will my tattoo artist actually use these designs?+

Most artists welcome detailed reference designs — it saves them hours of free consultation work. They'll usually adapt and refine your TattooDesignr designs to fit your body and their style. Show up with a clear vision; they'll do the rest.

What if I don't like any of the 8 designs?+

Email us within 24 hours and we'll regenerate a fresh set with refined prompts — no charge. If you're still unhappy, full money-back. We don't argue. Tattoos are forever; the design has to feel right.

Do I own the designs? Can I use them commercially?+

Yes. Full commercial license is included. Tattoo them, share them, sell them, frame them, get them inked on a hundred clients. They're yours. We make zero claim.

What about reference photos I upload?+

Reference photos are uploaded to our server, used for generation, and deleted within minutes. We never persist them, never train on them, and never share them. Our database literally has no table for user-uploaded content.

How is TattooDesignr different from ChatGPT or Midjourney?+

Generic AI tools give you generic art. We've fine-tuned dedicated models for each tattoo tradition — your Japanese designs follow Irezumi conventions; your minimalist linework actually translates to skin; your blackwork has the right contrast for healing. Tattoo design is a specialty. We treat it as one.

How long does generation take?+

Average 25-40 seconds for 8 designs. You'll see them appear one by one as they finish.

Will the design look good on my body specifically?+

TattooDesignr generates the design. Your tattoo artist adapts it to your body — that's their craft. Treat our designs as references, not final templates.

Is the design medically safe to tattoo?+

Your tattoo artist is the final authority on what can be safely tattooed. See our disclaimer for full details.

Where do I get the actual tattoo done?+

Take your designs to any licensed tattoo artist. Coming soon: artist directory + booking.
